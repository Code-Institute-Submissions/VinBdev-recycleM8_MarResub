Read about RecycleM8:

Who is Recycle M8?

RecycleM8 was founded by a group of environmentalists who are committed to making a more stable and safe environment by bringing practical habitual changes to how humans dispose of waste.

Why is Recycle M8 here?

RecycleM8's purpose is to show and educate any citizen trying to educate themselves or others about various types of waste and how to effectively dispose of this waste,

How old is Recycle M8?

We are a brand new non-profit organisation that actively encourages every single person to contribute in any way possible.

What is the future of Recycle M8?

The future of recycleM8 is to continually grow and eventually get to a development stage where users can create an account on the site as well as enabling a scanning capability. So users can scan bar codes of waste materials to fund out how to dispose of the waste. They will also have a user profile which will actively encourage users to share their recycling efforts amongst their own networks to help encourage others to also dispose of waste efficiently.
